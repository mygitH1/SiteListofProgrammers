Programmers

!The reason the computer technology have made progress in such leaps and bounds is due to the contributions of many people. However, the secret heroes are the programmers. They are the ones who come up with the marvellous programmers that bring human imagination to mind. They design games, utilities, wordprocessors, graphic edition softwares and a whole bunch of other softwares without which we can hardly survive today. In a way, they make the world keep moving. But barring one or two (Bill Gates and Steve Jobs come to mind), we hardly know about anyone else. So this is my tribute to the 20 forgotten genius programming minds now. There are probably a lot more, but that would take a book.
